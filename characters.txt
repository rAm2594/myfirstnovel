Balin: Dwarf companion of Thorin Oakenshield in The Hobby. Slain during an attempt to retake Moria.
Bard the Bowman: Man of Esgaroth who slayed Smaug the dragon.
Beorn: Skin-changer who resides in the region of Mirkwood and takes the form of a great bear.
Bilbo Baggins: A hobbit adventurer. Discovered the One Ring after its loss by Golluma.
Boromir: Member of the Fellowship of the Ring, son and heir to Denethor II of Gondor. Slain by Uruk-hai at Amon Hen.
Sauron: The title character and the primary antagonist, through the forging of the One Ring, of J. R. R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings, where he rules the land of Mordor and has the ambition of ruling the whole of Middle-earth
